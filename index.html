What Ligo AI Solves and Provides

Making use of machine learning and blockchain technologies, Ligo AI help businesses to enhance cybersecurity, automate daily tasks, optimize supply chain management, analyze and predict sales, improve customer service, augment their marketing strategies, improve talent management and more.

| Services

Core Offerings

Enhanced Cybersecurity

Ligo AI is an advanced technology solution designed to enhance the security of your business by identifying and proactively preventing cyber threats. By leveraging cutting-edge machine learning algorithms, Ligo AI can analyze large amounts of data and identify potential security vulnerabilities, allowing your organization to take swift action to protect sensitive information and maintain the integrity of your systems. This powerful tool is essential for any business looking to stay ahead of the ever-evolving cyber threat landscape.

Augmented Marketing

Ligo AI is providing powerful tools you need to analyze data and create effective marketing campaigns by leveraging advanced machine learning algorithms. Our AI technology can provide your business valuable insights about consumer behavior and preferences, helping you to create a more personalized and effective marketing campaigns by analyzing data and identifying the most effective content and strategies. This can help your business to reach your target audience more effectively and improve the overall marketing ROI.

Talent Management

Ligo AI can be used to improve HR and talent management by analyzing resumes, job applications, and candidate data to identify top candidates for a position. Our AI tools can also be used to screen resumes, schedule interviews or analyze existing employee data to identify patterns and predict the potential of your employees, helping you to identify high-performers. This can save HR professionals time and reduce the risk of human bias in the hiring process and to create more effective development and training programs.
